Causal Marketplace Experimentation Platform

Full-stack A/B experimentation platform on a simulated fashion-electronics marketplace, demonstrating where naive A/B testing fails under seasonal confounding and applying causal inference to recover true treatment effects.

  • Simulated 10K users, 800 products, and 200K interactions with seasonal confounding — naive A/B testing falsely concluded control won, missing $15,657 in estimated revenue
  • Applied Difference-in-Differences (+1.37pp causal lift) and Synthetic Control (peak lift +2.96pp in December) — decision flipped from "kill variant" to "ship it"
